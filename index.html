Welcome to aRmed Combat. A near future world of underground dualing where the augmented, known as 'Shoulders', pit their self-made, armored prosthetics against one another in a test of design, durability, and destructive capabilty. In this demo you will be able to create and then pit your own creation against those already in use, or deploy a pre-made aRm and get straight to the action!

    To build a custom aRm:
  • Select an avatar on the right, fill out the character sheet on the left, and then click

    Create!

    To use a pre-made aRm:
  • Select an avatar and click

    Deploy!
